The History

Donatello Scarsi first developed culinary skills producing and selling cheese. It was in Naples, however—the birthplace of pizza just an hour away from his hometown—where Scarsi went to study the art and craft of the pizzaiolo.

The Chef

Donatello Scarsi is a 38 year old chef from Pontinia, Italy. After learning all things Neapolitan, among some of the best pizza makers in the world, Scarsi came to the United States and opened several successful pizzerias before settling in Little Rock Arkansas.

The Food

Donatello Scarsi makes the best artisanal pizza on any college campus in the US. Only using the freshest ingredients, he hand tosses the pizza dough every morning. His food has won many awards throughout Europe and America.
